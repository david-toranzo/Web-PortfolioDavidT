About me


I'm David Toranzo. I'm a Unity developer, computer science student and a passionate about videogames. I started studying C# on my own when I was 16 years old. Since then, I made some games and learned more about games and how to produce them. In the last year I learned about new ways of programming (TDD, SOLID, design patterns) and I started to work on projects with multidisciplinary teams (in jams and small projects).

Entry for the GMTK Game Jam 2021, it fits the theme (Joined Together) because you're fate is tied to both the host and the queen parasite. In Parasite, you play as a spore.
